Welcome to Hold My Beer (HMB), an all-in-one app designed for solo handymen to run their entire business.

HMB is free to use and offers community-based support via GitHub issues and discussions.

Currently available on Android. iOS support is planned (contributors needed for App Store publishing).

Features

  • Customer database with multiple contacts/sites
  • Supplier database
  • Job management with subtasks and checklists
  • Time tracking
  • Job-specific shopping and packing lists
  • Quoting and invoicing
  • Job scheduling
  • Photo and receipt capture
  • Tool asset register
  • Xero integration for uploading invoices
  • In Development: SMS templates

Benefits

  • Feel more organized: all your customer, job, and supplier info in one place
  • Reduce trips: smart shopping and packing lists help reduce unnecessary travel
  • Bill every hour: easy time tracking means no lost billing opportunities
  • Work anywhere: offline-first design keeps you productive on remote sites
  • Less paperwork: create quotes and invoices on the go

Workflow Overview

HMB is built to follow the natural handyman workflow:

  • Receive a quote request
  • Conduct a site visit with notes and photos
  • Build an estimate and send a quote
  • Schedule the job
  • Prepare a shopping list and packing list
  • Track time on site
  • Invoice using fixed price or time & materials (including milestone support)

How It Works

Jobs are the core of HMB. Each job has:

  • Tasks, each with notes, status, estimates, and time entries
  • Annotated photos
  • Checklist items (shopping/tools/stock)

Limitations

HMB runs on a single Android device. That means:

  • Works offline
  • If the device is lost/damaged, so is your data
  • No team sync or multi-device support
